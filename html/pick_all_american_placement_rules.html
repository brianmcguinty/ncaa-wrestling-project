PICK ALL AMERICANS PLACEMENT RULES:

You are picking the top 8 spots (All Americans) in each weight class.

You only earn points for wrestlers who finish at the spot you predict.

If you pick a wrestler to come in 3rd and he finishes in any other spot you get no points. So if you pick a guy to come in 3rd and he comes in 1st you get no points.

If you pick a seeded wrestler to wrestle to his seed and he does indeed wrestle to his seed you receive 1 point. This means if you pick the man seeded 1st and he comes in 1st you receive 1 point, if you pick the man seeded 3rd to come in 3rd and he does indeed come in third you reciece 1 point.

If you correctly pick a seeded wrester to finish in a position that is not his seed you have to do some math. In this scenerio you will have 2 numbers, the wresters seed and their actual placement that you correctly picked. You take the smaller of the 2 numbers and subtract it from the bigger number to determine your points.

So if you correctly pick the number 1 seed to come in 8th place you receive 7 points. Placement value (8) is larger than the seed value (1) so subtract 8 - 1 = 7 points. If you picked the number 8 seed to finish 1st you also receive 7 points ( Seed value (8) subtracted from the placement value (1) ). If you pick the 3rd seed to come in 5th and he does finish in the 5 spot you receive 2 points. ( Placement value (5) - seed value (3) = 2 points ).

All unseeded wrestlers are considered to be seeded 15.
