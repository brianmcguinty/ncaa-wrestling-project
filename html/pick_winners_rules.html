PICK WINNERS RULES:

You are picking the winner in each weight class.

If your pick wins you get 8 points.

If your pick comes in 2nd you get 7 points.

If your pick comes in 3nd you get 6 points, etc, etc , though all the AA positions.

If your pick shows up at the 'show' but fails to AA you get -1.

If your pick gets injured before the 'show' or scratches for some reason you get a 0.
