PICK ALL AMERICANS PLACEMENT RULES:

You are picking the All Americans in each weight class in any order.

You earn points for every wrestler you you pick who finishes in one of the top 8 spots.

If a wrestler you picked is seeded 1 - 8 and finished in any of those spots you receive 1 point.

If a wrestler you picked is seeded 9 - 12 or is unseeded you receive 2 points if they finish in the top 8.
